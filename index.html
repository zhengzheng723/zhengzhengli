the wanderer
a young man moves through golden fields, bare stone walls, and empty institutional corridors — each space a different kind of silence. the clothes he wears are never costumes; they are skins. layered, architectural, sometimes dissolving into the landscape, sometimes standing apart from it. the shoot moves between colour and monochrome, between closeness and distance, tracing the quiet tension between who we are and where we find ourselves. nature does not frame him — it reads him.

not your category
she moves through colour like a declaration. fur the shade of fire, vinyl the weight of armour, neon against grey sky — nothing here is an accident. she skates, she strides, she holds a red bull and dares you to look away. and when the world turns monochrome, she doesn't disappear — she sharpens. between the fairground and the park bench, between the street and the studio, she is building a language that has no translation. the boot reads: i am not a girl. she already knew.

hello
i am zheng zheng
i am a fashion designer and art director with a deep passion for photography, travel, and visual storytelling. my work is driven by a fascination with the narratives behind images — the emotions, the journeys, and the authenticity they hold.

i believe that true stories, even if imperfect, carry a unique beauty that resonates more deeply than polished illusions. through design, styling, and imagery, i strive to create and capture moments that reflect this honesty, blending aesthetics with meaning to connect people through visual experiences.
